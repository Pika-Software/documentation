### Fix `cl_downloadfilter` problem {#fix-cl_download_filter}
FastDL content loading may be disabled for some users. 
Our FastDL automatically detects this and alerts users. 
To fix this problem, just enter the [console command](https://wiki.facepunch.com/gmod/Console_commands):
```
cl_downloadfilter all
```
*P.S. Don't forget to reconnect to the server to download the content*
